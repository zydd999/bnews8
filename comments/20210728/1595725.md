<h3>做法：</h3> <p>1、豆皮切丝，木耳用温水泡发切丝，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%e8%83%a1%e8%90%9d%e5%8d%9c/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 胡萝卜
