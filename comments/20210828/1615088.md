<p>当天傍晚5点48分，<a href="https://www.bannedbook.org/bnews/tag/%E7%82%B8%E5%BC%B9%E5%AE%A2/" class="st_tag internal_tag" rel="tag" title="标签 炸弹客 下的日志">炸弹客</a>用背心背了约25磅的<a
